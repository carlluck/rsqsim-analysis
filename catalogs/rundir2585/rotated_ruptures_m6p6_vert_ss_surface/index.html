(top)

  • Site [10 unique]
  • Joyner-Boore Distance [2 unique]
  • Rupture [100 unique]
  • Rupture Strike (Aki & Richards 1980 convention) [36 unique]

and the following quantities vary:

  • Path (from site to rupture centroid) [4 unique]
