## Full Spatial Distributions
*[(top)](#table-of-contents)*

These plots show ground motions across 27 ruptures. Each surface is sized slightely differently. The lengh range is [8.977422,20.702454] with a median length of 12.851756 (km). The width range is [10.996227,24.899773] with a median width of 19.547344 (km). The hypocenter distribution is shown below:

![hypo dist](resources/full_hypos.png)

Each column represents a defferent spectral period. The first row gives the log median ground motion (an average shakemap). The second row shows residuals at each point relative to the median ground motion at that distance (Rjb). Residuals are calculated individually for each ruprture, and then averaged spatially. The bottom row shows the standard deviation of log ground motions. The meidan surface outline is drawn with a dark cyan line, and the extents of all surfaces light cyan lines.
